A super quick, warming tofu curry soup with rice noodles 🍜

---

🔹 3 servings
🔹 Preparation time 15 min </br>

---

#### Ingredients

&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> ½ liter water </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 1 red onion </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 1 yellow bell pepper </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 1 large tomato </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 1 block of tofu </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 1 can of coconut milk </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> handful of dried mushrooms </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 2 tbsp curry paste </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> salt </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> nigella seeds </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> parsley </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> rice noodles (200 g) </br>

#### Preparation 

Add the chopped onion, pepper, tomato and tofu to boiling water in a pot. Add the coconut milk, mushrooms, curry paste, salt, and nigella seeds (season to taste).

Cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Place the noodles on plates, pour the soup over them, and serve with parsley (or not 😉).

---

Enjoy! 💙
