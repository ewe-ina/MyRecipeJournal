#### Ingredients

##### 🍫 Protein brownie

&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 1 tbsp coconut flour (10 g) </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 1 tbsp cocoa powder (7 g) </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 2 tbsp chocolate protein powder (15 g, Vegan Blend) </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 1 tbsp erythritol (10 g) </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 2 tsp rapeseed oil (10 ml) </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> a pinch of salt </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 5-6 tbsp water </br>

##### 🍓 Toppings

&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> a piece of dark chocolate </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> a few frozen raspberries </br>

#### Preparation

Mix everything (except the toppings) in a mug or ramekin with a spoon until you get a smooth batter.  
The amount of water depends on how moist you want your brownie to be.
(And if you want it extra chocolatey — sink a piece of chocolate into the batter.)

Microwave for 1 minute.
  
Highly recommended with frozen raspberries!

Enjoy! 💙
