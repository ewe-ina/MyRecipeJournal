A simple, filling fried rice with an egg‑like flavor from black salt 🍚

---

🔹 2 servings, 483 kcal each </br>

---

#### Ingredients

&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 200 g brown rice </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 1 tbsp rapeseed oil (15 ml) </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 1 red onion (110 g) </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 2 garlic cloves (10 g) </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 2 tbsp inactive yeast flakes (10 g) </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 1 tsp turmeric </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 1 tsp black salt (kala namak) </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 2 tbsp soy sauce (30 ml) </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 25 g radish sprouts </br>

#### Preparation

Cook the rice in lightly salted water (brown rice takes a bit longer).

Heat the oil in a pan. Add the diced onion and minced garlic. Sauté until golden. Add the cooked rice, nutritional yeast, turmeric, black salt, and soy sauce. (Why black salt? Because it has an eggy flavor 🙂)

Mix everything and fry for a moment. Serve on plates and top with sprouts.

---

Enjoy! 💙
