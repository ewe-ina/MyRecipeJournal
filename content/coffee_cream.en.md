#### Ingredients

&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 1 tbsp instant coffee (4 g) </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 1 tbsp erythritol (10 g) </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 1 tbsp ice-cold water (15 ml) </br>

#### Preparation

Mix all ingredients for a few minutes (a whisk is enough, especially for a small portion like this) until you get a thick cream. The colder the water, the better. I was curious whether it really works 😉 And it does! A cream appears — fluffy, intensely sweet, and strongly coffee‑flavored.

#### 🧪 Original recipe

&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 20 g instant coffee </br>
&emsp; <input type="checkbox"> 200 g sugar </br>

If you need an energy boost to stay up all night, this is definitely for you! 😉  
You can eat it as is or spread it on muffins. Prefer to drink it? Add cold water or milk. Want it hot? Mix with warm milk. Want it bitter? Skip the sweetener 😉
